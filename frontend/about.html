Our Story

During our 4th year attachment, our team worked under the innovative environment of Braincraft. There, we were encouraged to move beyond theory and focus on solving real-world problems with dedication and teamwork.

Through observation and research, we realized that choosing a personal hearing device can often feel complex and confusing. Hearing is deeply personal, yet recommendations are usually technical rather than user-centered..

This inspired us to create AudioIntel — a smart system designed to provide personalized hearing device recommendations based on individual needs, lifestyle, and hearing conditions. Instead of overwhelming users with specifications, AudioIntel delivers intelligent and simplified suggestions.

Built with commitment and collaboration under the guidance of Braincraft, AudioIntel reflects our growth, problem-solving mindset, and dedication to meaningful technology.
